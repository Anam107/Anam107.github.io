Afra Anam is a Lecturer and researcher in Civil Engineering. Her research interests include earthquake engineering, seismic assessment and retrofitting of existing structures, sustainable concrete technology, and durability of cementitious materials. She is also gradually exploring data-driven approaches and the application of machine learning in structural engineering.

This website presents her research interests, publications, academic activities, teaching experience, and curriculum vitae.
