--- layout: archive title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true --- Afra Anam is involved in undergraduate teaching in Civil Engineering, with a focus on structural analysis, mechanics of solids, engineering materials, and analysis-based courses. ## Courses Taught - Mechanics of Solids - Analysis of Statically Determinate Structures - Analysis of Statically Indeterminate Structures - ETABS-Based Structural Modeling and Analysis - Engineering Materials ## Teaching and Academic Activities Her teaching activities include classroom lectures, problem-solving sessions, laboratory instruction, academic advising, and supervision of undergraduate thesis work. She also assists students in thesis development, structural modeling, experimental data interpretation, technical writing, and presentation preparation. ## Thesis Supervision She supervises and assists undergraduate thesis projects related to structural engineering and construction materials. Her supervised areas include seismic assessment of reinforced concrete buildings, structural modeling and analysis, recycled aggregate concrete, high-performance concrete, ultra-high-performance concrete, and supplementary cementitious materials.
